About
June Jam

Since 1978, June Jam has been Delaware's premier outdoor concert experience, bringing together the best in rock, country, and blues under the open sky every summer.

What started as a small gathering of music lovers has grown into a beloved tradition, welcoming thousands of fans each year for a weekend of unforgettable performances, camping, community, and good times.

Rooted in the Delaware countryside, June Jam is more than a concert ‚Äî it's a homecoming.

Stay the Night

Camping

Wake up to music and make the most of your June Jam weekend with on-site camping. Our campgrounds are set in a comfortable field setting right near the action.

General Camping

Tent or small camper. Shared restroom facilities. Bring your own setup and enjoy a true festival camping experience.

RV / Electric Hookup

Limited electric hookup sites available for RVs and trailers. Reserve early ‚Äî these sell out fast!

Car Camping

Camp right next to your vehicle in designated car camping zones. Convenient and easy.

  • Campers may arrive Friday from 10am; depart by Sunday 6pm
  • Quiet hours enforced from 2am ‚Äì 8am
  • No fires ‚Äî charcoal grills and propane allowed

Marketplace

Vendors

June Jam's vendor marketplace is a beloved part of the festival experience. We welcome food vendors, artisans, craftspeople, and merchandise sellers to join us for the weekend.

Space is limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Applications open each spring. Fill out the vendor interest form to be notified when applications open.
